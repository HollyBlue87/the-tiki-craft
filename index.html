THE ULTIMATE PARTY EXPERIENCE

Feel the music, taste the flavour and embrace the spirit of Tobago aboard The Tiki Craft. With native Caribbean rhythms, freshly grilled BBQ, ice-cold drinks and ocean views all around, every journey becomes a floating island experience.
